Welcome to the 2023 ACT Auction!
The art auction is an online event run by the Climate Action Club in High School. It features nearly 40 sets of artwork that will be available for bidding over a period of several days. All proceeds will go to our charity, the Sustainable Favela Network.
In collaboration with the Sustainable Favela Network
How it works
Explore
Read more about our charity or details of the auction on our website.
Browse
Navigate to our “artworks” page to view all the amazing work creating for our event!
Bid
Fill out our Google Form to place a bid on one of the artworks. Highest bidders for each piece will be updated on the website daily. (Click here)
Collect
If you win any pieces , we will be in touch with you about how you can pay and pick up the artworks.
What is the ACT Club?

We are a non-profit organisation established in 2019 by High School students at the International School of Stavanger. We strive to promote awareness and raise funds for climate change through activities and initiatives pertaining to topics as well as issues surrounding environmental protection. Such topics can include recycling, deforestation, sustainability, waste management, pollution, biodiversity and mitigation of greenhouse gas emissions.
